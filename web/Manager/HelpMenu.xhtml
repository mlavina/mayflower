User Guide

Modify Employee

  1. Click on Manage Employee and follow instructions from there

Creating An Sales Report for a given Month

  1. Click on Revenue in the menu bar. Then click date
  2. Fill out the time range such that the time range is equal to a month
  3. Click View Report

Produce a list of Flights

  1. Click Flights and then Click all Flights
  2. If data does not show up, please refresh the page

List Reservation by Flight or Customer Name

  1. Click Reservation and then by Customer/Flight
  2. For reservation by customer, input a customer name and click customer
  3. For reservation by flight, input flight info in the form of airlineId flightNo or AA 111

Revenue Generated by Flight/City/Customer

  1. From the navigation bar, click on Revenue.
  2. In the dropdown that appears, click "By Flight/City/Customer"
  3. Enter a value in the textfield and click either flightnumber, city, or customer to view the revenue information
  4. For customer input customerId, for flight input form airlineId flightNo and for city enter the city

Customer and Employee who Generated the Most Revenue

  1. Go back to the Home page
  2. It is displayed here

Most Active Flights

  1. Click flights and then active flights

Customers who have seats on flights

  1. Click on reservation and then reserved seats by flight
  2. Input flight info in form of AA 111 and then click flight

Flights from an Airport

  1. Click flights and then airport flight
  2. Enter the name of a city and click flight

Late and On Time Flights

  1. Go to Home Screen, it is there
